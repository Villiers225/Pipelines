Glossary (acronyms used on this page)

  • Find a Tender service (FTS) – The United Kingdom platform for regulated procurement notices.
  • Contracts Finder – The central United Kingdom portal for opportunities above £12,000.
  • Defence Sourcing Portal (DSP) – The Ministry of Defence e‑sourcing system.
  • Single Source Regulations Office (SSRO) – Regulator for qualifying non‑competitive defence contracts.
  • Qualifying Defence Contract (QDC) / Qualifying Sub‑contract (QSC) – Contracts subject to the single‑source framework.
  • Defence Contract Analysis and Reporting System (DefCARS) – Reporting system for SSRO contracts.
  • Crown Commercial Service (CCS) – Central buying body that lets cross‑government frameworks (for example RM‑coded agreements).
  • RM code – Crown Commercial Service framework identifier (for example RM6098).
  • North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) – Intergovernmental military alliance.
  • International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) – United States export control rules for defence items and technical data.
  • Other Transaction Authority (OTA) – United States authority for research and prototype agreements outside standard procurement statutes.
